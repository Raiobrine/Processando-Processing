This site archives the work of Casey REAS from 2001–present. REAS is an artist working in Los Angeles, a professor at UCLA, and a co-founder of Processing and the Processing Foundation. In the tradition of the 90s WWW, this site is always a work in progress and rough around the edges. For more regular updates, check out the studio INSTAGRAM, TWITTER, and VIMEO. (We suggest the alternate Caesuras INSTAGRAM and TWITTER for the good stuff.) See the INFORMATION page for more links, contact info, and data.
